Detailed Curriculum

Week-by-Week Breakdown

Here's exactly what you'll learn each week — no vague promises, just a clear roadmap.

Week 1

Email Marketing Core & Setup

Build a rock-solid foundation in email marketing from scratch. No prior experience needed.

  • Understanding email marketing as a high-income skill
  • Setting up Klaviyo / Mailchimp accounts from scratch
  • Building your first email list (lead magnets, opt-in pages)
  • Email deliverability essentials — SPF, DKIM, DMARC explained
  • Types of email campaigns: Welcome, promotional, nurture sequences
  • Hands-on: Create and send your first email campaign
Week 2

High-Converting Copywriting & Automated Flows

Learn to write emails people actually open, read, and click on — then automate the entire process.

  • Direct response copywriting fundamentals
  • Subject line formulas that get 40%+ open rates
  • Writing CTAs that drive real clicks and conversions
  • Setting up automated email flows (abandoned cart, welcome series)
  • A/B testing strategies for continuous improvement
  • Hands-on: Write a complete 5-email welcome sequence
Week 3

Fiverr Domination & Gig SEO

Turn Fiverr into a reliable client acquisition channel with optimized gigs and winning proposals.

  • Complete Fiverr profile setup (bio, skills, portfolio)
  • Gig SEO — finding profitable keywords your buyers search for
  • Creating gig images and descriptions that convert
  • Writing proposals that actually win orders
  • Handling client communication like a professional
  • Hands-on: Create 3 optimized gigs and send your first proposals
Week 4

Direct Client Hunting & Invoicing

Go beyond Fiverr. Find high-paying clients directly and manage your freelancing like a business.

  • LinkedIn prospecting — finding and connecting with decision-makers
  • Cold email outreach — templates, follow-ups, and timing
  • Using tools like Hunter.io and Instantly.ai to find client emails
  • Setting up professional invoicing and payment collection
  • Building long-term client relationships for recurring income
  • Hands-on: Send your first 20 cold outreach messages to real prospects
Week 5

AI UGC Ads — The Future of Advertising

Master the hottest skill in digital marketing — creating AI-generated UGC ads that brands are paying premium for.

  • Understanding UGC ads — why brands pay $500+ per video
  • AI avatar tools — HeyGen, Synthesia, and alternatives
  • Writing viral ad scripts — hooks, pain points, CTAs
  • AI voiceover generation for authentic-sounding ads
  • Editing & stitching AI UGC videos professionally
  • Hands-on: Create 3 complete AI UGC ads from scratch
